50 mM Tris pH 8.0 10 mM EDTA






Requirements
------------
50 mM 1 M Tris pH 8.0 5 ml
1 mM 0.5 M EDTA 2 ml 
H2O up to 100 ml 


Method
------

- Combine Tris & EDTA to final volume of 7ml

- Add H2O up to 100 ml 

    Store at 4'C
