.. sectionauthor:: Martin Fitzpatrick <martin.fitzpatrick@gmail.com>

1M MgSO4






Requirements
------------
24.074 g MgSO4


Method
------

- Add MgSO4 to suitable container. Make up to 200ml with distilled water.

    Store at room temperature
