.. tags:: glycine,hcl,media-solutions

0.1 M Glycine-HCl, pH 2.7






Requirements
------------
11.1 g Glycine-HCl
800 ml dH2O


Method
------

- Combine 11.1 g Glycine-HCl with 800 ml dH2O

- Adjust pH to 2.7 and bring volume to 1 L with dH2O
