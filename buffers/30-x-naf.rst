30 x NaF






Requirements
------------
378 mg NaF (0.9 M NaF)
10ml distilled water


Method
------

- Add 378 mg NaF (0.9 M NaF) to a suitable container. 



- Add distilled water up to a final volume of 10 ml 

*Store at -20oC*
