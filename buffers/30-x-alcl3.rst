30 x AlCl3






Requirements
------------
20 mg (1.5 mM AlCl3)
100ml distilled water


Method
------

- Add 20 mg (1.5 mM AlCl3) to a suitable container.  


- Make up to 100ml with distilled water. Store until further use.

*Store at room temperature.*
