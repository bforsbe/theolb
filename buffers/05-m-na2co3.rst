0.5 M Na2CO3

0.5 M Na2CO3






Requirements
------------
531 mg Na2CO3
10ml distilled water


Method
------

- Add 531 mg Na2CO3 to a suitable container. 


- Make up to 10ml with distilled water. Store at room temperature until further use.

*Store at room temperature.*
